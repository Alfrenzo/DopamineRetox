## 🎮 About the Game

**Dopamine Retox** is a Roblox experience built as a satirical love letter — and a brutal critique — of the modern "brainrot" game genre.

Your screen is cluttered with useless information. Numbers are going up for no reason. Popups won't stop appearing. You can't look away.

Sound familiar?

This game takes everything that modern hyper-stimulation games do to keep you hooked, cranks it up to 11, and holds a mirror up to the player. It's chaotic, it's overwhelming, and that's entirely the point.

---

## ✨ Features

- 📊 **Numbers Going Up** — XP, coins, combo multipliers, totally meaningless stats. All going up. Always.
- 💥 **Constant Popups** — Achievement unlocked! Daily reward! New quest! You blinked!
- 🌀 **Screen Clutter** — UI elements fighting for your attention at all times
- 🎰 **Infinite Dopamine Loop** — Designed to feel rewarding while meaning absolutely nothing
- 😵 **Satirical Commentary** — A tongue-in-cheek jab at games engineered to exploit your attention
